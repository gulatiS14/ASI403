Detection Steps for Protein

(Steinberg, 2009)
  1. Sample Preparation

    1. Weigh 1.0 g of soil and transfer it to a sterile beaker.

    2. Prepare a solution with 50% methanol and 10% acetic acid. Add 10 mL of this solution to the soil sample.

  2. Staining with SYPRO® Ruby Protein Gel Stain:

    • Apply a small amount of SYPRO® Ruby protein gel stain to the soil sample. Leave it for 5 minutes.

  3. Fluorescence Detection

    1. Detect the fluorescence using a fluorescence spectrometer.

    2. Set the excitation wavelength to 450–488 nm and the emission wavelength to 610 nm to detect the fluorescence.

Reagent Required:

  1. Methanol
  2. Acetic Acid
  3. SYPRO® Ruby protein gel stain
